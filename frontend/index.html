Robustness and Faithfulness

A major gap in current XAI literature is the lack of standardized metrics to measure the faithfulness of explanations. Current attribution methods (e.g., LIME, SHAP) are shown to be highly sensitive to adversarial perturbations, leading to fragile explanations that humans might misinterpret as robust. 1

Human-Centric Challenges

Explanations often ignore user expertise and cognitive load. There is a strong call in recent papers for interdisciplinary work involving cognitive science to calibrate user trust effectively. 2 3
